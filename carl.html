Our Mission: We aim to raise awareness, provide cyber safety practices, and engage communities in combating TFGBV while supporting marginalized groups, including PWDs, through inclusive resources and advocacy.
Our Vision: To create a world where young women, girls, and persons with disabilities are empowered to navigate digital spaces safely, confidently, and free from Technology-Facilitated Gender-Based Violence (TFGBV).
Our History: Proud alumni of AGCCI by UN Women. After the coding camp, some joined Siemens EmpowerHER and received scholarships in cybersecurity. Shield Maidens was born to empower more young women.
