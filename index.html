Our Mission: We aim to raise awareness, provide cyber safety practices, and engage communities in combating TFGBV while supporting marginalized groups, including PWDs, through inclusive resources and advocacy.
Our Vision: To create a world where young women, girls, and persons with disabilities are empowered to navigate digital spaces safely, confidently, and free from Technology-Facilitated Gender-Based Violence (TFGBV).
Our History: Proud alumni of AGCCI by UN Women. After the coding camp, some joined Siemens EmpowerHER and received scholarships in cybersecurity. Shield Maidens was born to empower more young women.

About Us

Shield Maidens was founded in 2024 by Sumaiyya Jumma and Maria Muneeni as a way of giving back to the community. As beneficiaries of UN Women, the African Girls Can Code Initiative (AGCCI), and Siemens EmpowerHER, they recognized the need to create a platform dedicated to advocacy, empowerment, and education. At Shield Maidens, our mission is to educate young women and youth on digital safety while actively fighting against technology-facilitated gender-based violence (TFGBV). We are committed to fostering an inclusive world by advocating for gender equality, justice, and the protection of online spaces. Our goal is to empower individuals with the knowledge and tools needed to navigate the digital world safely, to amplify voices against gender-based violence, and to drive meaningful change through advocacy and education. Together, we stand for a safer, more inclusive future for all.

Learn to Be Safe Online

Strong Passwords

Use unique and strong passwords for your accounts. Enable two-factor authentication for extra security.

Avoid Phishing

Don't click on unknown links or emails. Verify sender identities before sharing personal information.

Privacy Settings

Keep your social media accounts private. Limit who can see your personal information.

Seek Help

If you experience online threats, report them immediately to the authorities or trusted organizations. Please contact Us or Get in touch with one of our Team
