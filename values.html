Our Core Values

At Shield Maidens, we are defenders of digital dignity. Our initiative empowers young women, girls, and marginalized communities to navigate online spaces with confidence, strength, and safety. Guided by our core values, we stand united against Technology-Facilitated Gender-Based Violence (TFGBV) and advocate for a more inclusive, just, and fearless digital world.

Empowerment

We uplift young women, girls, and marginalized groups to lead confidently and safely in digital spaces, fighting against TFGBV every step of the way.

Integrity

At Shield Maidens, We commit to honesty, transparency, and ethical action, ensuring trust within our community and beyond.

Advocacy

We amplify voices against technology-facilitated violence, fighting for justice and safer, more inclusive online environments.

Solidarity

We stand united with survivors, communities, and allies to promote resilience, healing, and collective action against TFGBV.

Inclusion

We ensure everyone — including persons with disabilities — has a safe space to learn, grow, and thrive in the digital world.

Join Us in Making a Difference

At Shield Maidens, we believe in the power of collective action. Together, we can create a safer, more inclusive digital space for all. Get involved today to help us fight against Technology-Facilitated Gender-Based Violence and advocate for the rights of marginalized communities online.
