JOIN OUR MISSION TO COMBAT TECH-FACILITATED GENDER-BASED VIOLENCE

LET'S BUILD SAFER DIGITAL SPACES, TOGETHER.

We are proud to launch a heartfelt initiative rooted in equity, safety, inclusion and community. Our mission is to raise awareness, promote effective cyber safety practices, and foster meaningful community engagement in the fight against Technology Facilitated Gender-Based Violence (TFGBV).

At the core of our work is a commitment to inclusivity, especially for individuals from marginalized communities, including People with Disabilities (PWDs). Through accessible resources, advocacy, and partnerships, we strive to ensure that no one is left behind in the journey toward safer online and offline environments.

With the support of corporate partners, philanthropists, and passionate changemakers, we're shaping a future where technology empowers rather than endangers, and where every voice can be heard without fear.
